Don't know your size?

If you aren't able to use the ring size chart because you don't know your size, follow these simple steps to find out what ring size you are.

Find My Ring Size

  • Find a piece of fabric that isn't thick and doesn't stretch
  • Wrap the fabric around the finger you'd like to wear a ring on
  • Use a pen or marker to mark where the the end meets
  • Use your ruler of choice, from your country, and measure the distance
  • Use the chart above to translate the size to another country's size if needed

What You Need:

1. String

2. Ruler

3. Yourself!

How to Measure the Ring Size Correctly

    If you're new to ring sizing techniques or ordering ring sizes online, these are important things to keep in mind. Once you've measured your ring finger, or whatever finger you want to wear the ring on, check for these things.

  • Does the measurement fit your finger, and over the finger's knuckle?
  • Did you measure when your hands felt normal and were not swollen?
  • Did you measure in the later evening? If not, you should.
  • Each of your fingers is unique, never choose the size of a similar finger.

How to Measure a Ring's Size

You can use the same steps mentioned here to measure a ring you already have at home too. Be sure before ordering to keep in mind whether or not you're measuring a thick or thin band. You may find that for thicker bands, you need more room for the knuckle area. Whenever possible, measure a ring in a similar style to the one you want to buy.
